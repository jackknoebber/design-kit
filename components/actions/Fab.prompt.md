Material 3 Floating Action Button — the screen's single most important action, floating over content.

```jsx
<Fab icon="add" />
<Fab icon="edit" color="tertiary" size="large" />
<Fab icon="add" extended label="Compose" />
```

`size` small/regular/large; `color` primary/secondary/tertiary/surface; `extended` + `label` for the pill form; `lowered` drops elevation to level 1.
